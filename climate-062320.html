This Channel and the Climate
June 23, 2020

Effective September 1, 2022:
The YouTube channel and all other films will not utilize private transportation (such as personal cars) to assist in the production of any film. This includes pre-production transportation to the film location and as part of the film. This date will be reviewed no later than July 31, 2022.

Effective June 23, 2020:
This channel and related content will not promote the usage of personal cars and private transportation, due to the negative impacts on the world's climate. Ben will stop driving a car on the date that the previous statement (banning vehicles in the film process) goes into effect.
