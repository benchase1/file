'''Ben Chase''' is a [[filmmaker]] from the [[U.S. state]] of [[Massachusetts]]. He is most notable for his videos released on [[YouTube]].

== Early life ==
Chase was born on 17 October 2003 in Massachusetts. In March 2009, Chase became a transportation enthusiast. 

Chase began doing community service work in his town in June 2016, and participated in ''Young Neighbors in Action'' in July 2019.

Chase set school history when he became the school's youngest and longest-serving member of their student leadership team.
<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.benchase.tk/about|title=About - Ben Chase|author=Ben Chase|date=|access-date=20 May 2020}}</ref>

== Film career ==
=== Original channel (2014-2017) ===
Chase began making YouTube videos on 17 January 2014, when he uploaded his first video of him playing ''[[Minecraft]]''. He deleted his original channel on 25 August 2017 after pressure from his family. He had 73 subscribers and very little views on his old channel.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.benchase.tk/creations/channel|title=YouTube Channel - Ben Chase|author=Ben Chase|date=|access-date=20 May 2020}}</ref>

=== Current channel (2017-present) ===
On 1 September 2017, Chase restarted his channel, to create better content.

== Additional occupations ==
=== Music career ===
Chase and a friend began creating an the album ''Fork'' on 7 November 2018, and the album released for download on 14 April 2019. Subsequent albums ''Fork 2: The Unnamed Sequel'' and ''Fork 3: Quarantine Edition'' released on 23 August 2019 and 22 May 2020, respectively.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.benchase.tk/creations/fork|title=Fork - Ben Chase|author=Ben Chase|date=|access-date=20 May 2020}}</ref>

=== Blogging ===
On 23 September 2019, Chase posted as a guest on the transportation blog ''Miles in Transit'', reviewing [[Grafton station (MBTA)]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://milesintransit.com/2019/09/23/guest-post-grafton/|title=GUEST POST: Grafton|author=Miles Taylor|date=23 September 2019|access-date=20 May 2020}}</ref>

On 18 May 2020, Chase joined the transportation blog ''On the Rails'', because of his interest in the [[New York City Subway]] system.
