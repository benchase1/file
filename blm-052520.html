Black Lives Matter
May 25, 2020

Today, in Minneapolis, Minnesota, United States, a man by the name of George Floyd was killed by local police officers. (This is in the news, so I won't go into much detail.)

Since the channel started in 2014 (and since I was born in 2003), myself and my videos have shown support for everyone, no matter what race (or gender. wealth, education, etc.) they are. I have witnessed police brutality myself, and it is not fair what happens to people with a different race than themselves. The United States has had a long history of discrimination towards Black people, and I do not support this.
